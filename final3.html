Frequently Asked Questions

Common Queries

How do I book an appointment?

Navigate to the "Book Appointment" section, select your location, choose a doctor, and complete payment via card, UPI, or net banking.

Can I order medicines without a prescription?

A valid prescription is required to order medicines through our platform.

How does the disease prediction work?

Enter your symptoms in the Disease Prediction section, and our system will analyze them to suggest possible conditions.

Can I cancel an appointment?

Yes, appointments can be canceled through the Book Appointment section by contacting support.

Is my data secure?

We use advanced encryption to protect your personal and medical information.

How do I access emergency consultations?

Go to the "Emergency" section to connect with a doctor via chat, voice call, or video call for urgent care.
